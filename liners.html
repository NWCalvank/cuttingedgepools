Liner Replacement Process

When replacing a pool liner, it's important to get you back to enjoying your pool as quickly as possible. With 7 years of experience serving London, ON pool and concrete needs, Steve and the Cutting Edge team make your liner replacement process quick and comfortable.

The first step for a pool liner replacement is to remove the old liner. This requires draining the pool. With the water removed, the team checks for any small repairs that should be made and takes accurate measurements with which the new liner will be made.

You can rest comfortably, knowing that your liner measurements are being processed by the manufacturer within a few days of our first visit. If you would like any other repairs, restorations, or concrete work done, the best time for these projects is often while your pool liner is out.

Discuss any extra work that you would like done with the team early to ensure that your projects will be completed in a timely manner.

Once the liner is manufactured, the Cutting Edge team will deliver and install it in your pool. If you'd prefer to have water trucks brought in to save you filling your pool with a garden hose, we are happy to provide that service as well.

Overall, this process can take 2-3 weeks. We recommend booking your 2018 liner replacement early, ensuring that you'll be ready to swim when the weather is ready for it.

Our liners are made with high-quality, Canadian, virgin vinyl. While other companies settle for a 'bag fit' that stretches to the exact shape of your pool, we choose to cut a liner to your pool's specifications. This increases the lifespan of the liner and prevents unnecessary visual damage.
